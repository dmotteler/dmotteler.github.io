Quick start:
  1. Select venue in 'Songs for'.
  2. Adjust date if needed.
  3. Select previous event or "select new list" in 'Use:'.
  4. To change the songs listed:
    • Select categories, then click on the '+'.
    • Songs will be added to the cheat sheet in the order chosen.
    • Setting the checkbox for a song will add the song to the end of the cheat sheet.
    • Clearing the checkbox for a song removes it from the cheat sheet.
    • When finished, click the "Done" button at the top of the page.
  5. Move a song by click-hold on the song, drag to new position, release.
  6. Remove a song by click-hold on the song, drag off the table, release.
  7. I guess you found the help button?
  8. Click download arrow when done.
