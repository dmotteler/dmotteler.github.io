Quick start:
  1. Select venue in 'Songs for'.
  2. Adjust date if needed.
  3. Select previous event or enter a new list in 'Use:'.
  4. Add songs with down arrow. Multiple songs may be selected. Songs are added to the bottom of the table.
  5. Move a song by click-hold on the song, drag to new position, release.
  6. Remove a song by click-hold on the song, drag off the table, release.
  7. I guess you found the help button?
  8. Click download arrow when done.
